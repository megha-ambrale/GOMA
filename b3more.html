Energy Efficient Process Pumps By Goma





Over the period of years, technology has made myriads of advances. The very purpose of its invention was to make a man’s life easier. However, optimum use of energy is crucial in the industrial sector. There should be a perfect balance between energy consumption by the machinery and the result it yields. To make various industrial processes easier and to increase efficiency, energy-efficient process pumps were created.



Our high-pressure pumps are most resourceful & highly efficient for various applications like testing, spraying, cutting, cleaning, descaling, transferring of fluids, pressure injection, etc. These energy-efficient Process Pumps are used to move fluid in an industrial process. They can be any assortment of pump types depending on the application. They can be either heavy-duty centrifugal pumps used to pump acidic or rasping fluids. Or, they can also be positive displacement pumps.



Goma manufactures a variety of process pumps like the plunger pumps which are either Triplex or Quintuplex. You can choose from over 15 different models depending upon power ratings. The pumps are available in electric as well as engine-driven motors with various options of coupling & mounting as per the customer’s application need. A variety of cleaning accessories and other accessories such as Force speed lubrication of power end, oil cooler for power end, pulsation suppressors, etc. are also available here.



There are various types of process pumps. API 674 Process Pumps are the pumps that are used in hydrocarbon processing plants and refineries. These pumps cover a number of centrifugal pump types such as vertical turbine pumps, end suction pumps, multi-stage pumps, horizontal split case pumps, and others.



A centrifugal pump uses an impeller to move water and other fluids. On the other hand, a positive displacement pump makes a fluid move by trapping a fixed amount and forcing that rapt volume into the discharge pipe.



There are a lot of energy-efficient processes pumps available in the market today. These are easy to use and make industrial work a lot easier and more effective. Goma has a wide range of pumps at extremely reasonable prices that cater to various industrial needs, big or small.



An industrial plant is not built overnight. It takes a lot of effort and brain in dealing with the complexities of technology. Nevertheless, Goma has proved to be a boon by making the best appliances available to us.
