How does a Goma process Almond Milk in an economical way?





What is Almond Milk?


Almond Milk is a vegan alternative to regular milk. Revenue for the Almond Milk Production industry is expected to grow over the next 2 to 3 years, as demand for alternative dairy beverages continues to strengthen. It is speculated that the food producers will face competition from organic milk, adventurous consumers, as well as consumers with dietary restrictions will increasingly demand industry products made by Almond Milk.


What are the benefits of Almond Milk?


Almond Milk is a great source of protein, which is present in the muscles, skin, and various other organs and which helps the body to maintain and repair itself.


It contains riboflavin, which is a form of vitamin B that has been proven to work in conjunction with other nutrients like iron to regulate muscle strength and growth.


It also has antioxidants that assist in repairing damaged skin, as well as regulating vitamin A.


How Almond Milk is processed?

  • Almonds are soaked first
  • Then they are ground and the milk is extracted.
  • High-quality natural flavoring agents are then blended with it
  • Sterilized and packaged
