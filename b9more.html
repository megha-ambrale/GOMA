Everything You Should Know about Crate Washer Before Buying.





What is a Crate washer?

A crate washer is a large industrial stainless-steel machine used for washing and sanitizing crates and baskets, suitable for all sectors of production and ensuring impeccable washing for any type of crate. The crate washing systems are fully automated and designed according to extremely high quality protocols, as well as being designed and constructed specifically for customer requirements to increase efficiency and productivity. It is used to clean plastic storage crates. This equipment quickly and efficiently cleans them.



How does a Crate Washer work?

In this machine, the crates are moved ahead through the washer using synthetic chains with spraying arcs accompanied by synthetic click nozzles. The crates are cleaned thoroughly in the main wash zone which has water served by an energy-efficient pump. This main wash tank has sensors to detect the water levels which makes the job easier for the machine operator. This equipment is very easy to handle and operate, which makes it the best choice for the setups that need crate washers for their businesses.



Many industries like dairy, fruit processing, and vegetable industry need crates in their operations, hence, the level of hygiene must be maintained carefully.



GOMA’s manufacturing process includes the feature where the crates/trays are washed thoroughly by a high pressure water jetting. This means it doesn’t require steam, detergent, or other chemicals to clean it. This leads to savings in cost and energy, when compared to the conventional crate washers.



General arrangement & technology of operation

We customize the crate washer according to the requirement The machine is designed, developed, and configured for each individual customer, on the basis of their requests and requirements. (It can be Single or Double Track)



The crates/trays are conveyed automatically by conveyor chain fitted on stainless steel fabricated frame with transparent polycarbonate sheet. This allows the clear inspection the of Crates/Trays..


The crates/trays pass through a specially designed High Pressure Water Jet Nozzle having an accurate spray angle for washing and removing the most stubborn residue from crates/trays. This is done to facilitate cleanliness and hygiene.


At GOMA, we manufacture machines in which the high pressure is generated by the pump driven through an electric motor and mounted close to the conveyor.


We have been manufacturing these machines for a really long time. That assures the reliability and performance levels, which remained unchanged over the years.


Benefits of the Crate Washer:
  • It is the most hygienic method of crate cleansing.
  • Easy to use – A single person can operate this machine.
  • All stainless steel, which means no problem with rusts.
  • Improves employee safety – it is one of our prime mottos.
  • It facilitates a reduction in maintenance costs and the time taken for the process.
  • Reduces costs.
  • Comes with easily adjustable click nozzles.
  • Our crate washer is extremely energy efficient.
