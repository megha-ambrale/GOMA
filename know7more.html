11 April 2022

Free Blood Donation & Thalassemia Organized in GOMA, Thane.






GOMA is always keen to take such initiatives that help our society to
become better tomorrow. GOMA joined hands with the Rotary Club of
Thane to organize a voluntary blood donation camp and Thalassemia test
of their employees and relatives.



Every tomorrow needs a blood donor today. However, within a few days of
blood donation, our body produces new fluids in the place of old ones.
We have successfully collected 50 units of blood during this voluntary
blood donation campaign that drives support and life to the needy ones.



We believe everyone should take a step towards the society to donate blood
because in India more than 4 crore blood units are in demand out of which
only 5 lakh units are only available. Our small steps can change or save
someone's life, that too with our benefit. Our benefit? Yes,
blood donation benefits us as well, let's dig into this.



Benefits of Blood Donation.


Prevents Hemochromatosis


Prevent Cancer


Helps to lose weight


Product new blood cells


Preserve good health of heart and liver



Well, along with the blood donation GOMA also has managed to organize a
free Thalassemia test camp for their employees and their relatives. First of all,
what is Thalassemia? This is an inherited blood disorder that can be passed by the parents,
in this disease a person is unable to generate enough protein which leads to a low rate
of Haemoglobin in the body.



What happens if you test positive for Thalassemia?


Thalassemia reduces the rate of Haemoglobin which causes Anemia. If you have Thalassemia,
these symptoms can be found in your body given below:


Fatigue
Weakness
Pale Skin
Slow Growth
Abdominal swelling
Dark Urine
Facial Bone Deformities


And most importantly if person A has mild Thalassemia and his/her partner
has mild Thalassemia then the chance of having Thalassemia positive kid increases
in the future. Thus, is it suggested to take a test of Thalassemia by doctors and we understand
the core of this pain. For the betterment of our employees and their friends & family, we took this
initiative to conduct a free Thalassemia test along with the blood donation camp.



No business can grow if their society can't grow. Thus, we goal to grow together
along with our healthy society.
GOMA has/is/will be serving its role towards society with honesty and responsibility.
