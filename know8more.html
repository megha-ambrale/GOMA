20 January 2022




Goma Group of Companies is honored to have been awarded the INNOVATION LEADER
IN DAIRY TECHNOLOGY by TPCI (Trade Promotional Council of India) MSME EXPORT EXCELLENCE AWARDS 2022.


GOMA Group of Companies was founded by Chairman Shri. K. M. Gosrani in 1982.
Goma has more than 4 decades of experience in designing, manufacturing, executing projects on
a Turnkey basis for, Dairy, Food, Juice & Beverage Processing in India & various countries of Africa,
the Middle East, and South Asia.



We have continuously strived to raise the standards in the dairy technology sector by developing the first Indigenous
High-Pressure Homogeniser back in 1991 and now a few years back we have developed the first UHT-Ultra Heat Treatment
(Make in India) for the extended long shelf life of milk and beverages, which is recognized by an eminent body
like the TPCI makes this even more prestigious. This has been a perfect start to the year for us, and we look
. forward to raising the standards and creating innovative products each year in line with the “Make in India” philosophy advocated by our honorable Prime Minister of India for the Dairy Industry.



We would like to thank various Organizations like the Indian Dairy Association(IDA), NDDB,
IDEA & our most valuable customers and suppliers in India and across the globe who have continuously
encouraged and supported us without which it would not have been
possible to reach such great heights in the field of machinery for Dairy Industry.
